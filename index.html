Core Skills

I'm Tom Claus, a freelancer specialized in front end development and social media integrations from Hasselt, Belgium (that little country between the UK, France and Germany).

In Januari 2012 I started as a freelancer to experiment with new technologies and social media on the web. In between I'm a technology evangelist for Mobile Vikings and CityLife.

I strongly believe that the web should be accessible from everywhere using every possible device out there. I've got years of experience with HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, jQuery, responsive webdesign, social media and much more.

If you'd like to work with me for a specific length of time or if you have a project you need help with, contact me below.

P.S - I have experience teaching front end development, HTML/CSS authoring and speaking to agencies and classes - these are some of my favourite things too.
